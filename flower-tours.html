Flower Tours

Flower Tours in Germany

Spring blossoms, summer gardens & flower festivals

Bloom season your way

When winter fades, Germany bursts into colour: orchards in bloom, flower festivals, themed gardens and leafy parks. We tailor each day for gentle walks, scenic viewpoints and relaxed café stops.

Two easy approaches

1) Base-and-daytrips around one region; 2) Or link 2–3 areas for a broader mosaic of blossoms and gardens.

Perfect combinations

Flower Tours pair beautifully with the Castles & Gardens itinerary and with Wine Tours for vineyard valleys in spring and summer.

  • Best time: Mar–Jun (spring blossoms) • Jul–Sep (summer gardens).
  • Where: Historic towns, parks and show gardens across Germany.
  • Style: Gentle pace • curated photo stops • cafés & viewpoints.
  • Custom: Private, tailor-made itineraries to match your time and interests.

Optional add-on: Netherlands tulip fields

Add a 1–2 day extension in the Netherlands during tulip season (roughly late Mar–May) to see bulb fields and famous gardens.
