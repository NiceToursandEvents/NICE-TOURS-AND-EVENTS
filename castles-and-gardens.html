Two dazzling ways to live Germany’s castle dream

Germany is a land of storybook castles, elegant palaces and sculpted gardens. Choose one of the routes below — or combine them — to match your dates and travel style. On request, we can extend the itinerary to the Netherlands for a touch of Dutch gardens and moated castles.

1) Castles & Gardens Escape

A refined journey through palaces with mirror-like lakes and baroque gardens. Think orangery-lined avenues, ornate fountains and hilltop fortresses with sweeping views.

  • Curated mix of palaces, romantic ruins and signature gardens.
  • Pace set to you: from leisurely strolls to photo-perfect viewpoints.
  • Optional spring add-on to the Netherlands for blooming gardens and classic castles.

2) German Castle Route (Burgenstraße)

Follow the historic Burgenstraße — the German Castle Route — linking dozens of castles and fortresses across charming medieval towns. Expect cobbled streets, timber-framed squares and river-valley panoramas.

  • Iconic castle views along a legendary route rich in history.
  • Flexible stages: choose shorter scenic hops or full-day drives.

Elevate your itinerary by pairing with the Romantic Rhine — home to cliffside castles — or add the German Wine Route for scenic vineyard drives and Riesling tastings. In spring, the Flower Tours make a charming seasonal addition.
