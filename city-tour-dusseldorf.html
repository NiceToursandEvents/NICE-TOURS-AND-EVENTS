City Tour in Düsseldorf

a refined walk through history and modern flair

Discover the vibrant city of Düsseldorf with a guided tour that blends rich cultural history and contemporary elegance. From the Altstadt to the Rhine promenade, the city reveals an unforgettable mix of tradition and innovation.

We’ll explore renowned galleries, historic churches and the chic Königsallee. There’s time for charming cafés and architectural highlights such as the media harbor (MedienHafen) and the Rheinturm — always at a comfortable, unhurried pace.

Looking for more? This city tour pairs beautifully with the Romantic Rhine, the region’s Castles & Gardens, or North Rhine-Westphalia icons like the awe-inspiring Cologne Cathedral and the idyllic hills of Bergisches Land. Elegant, flexible, and tailored to you.
