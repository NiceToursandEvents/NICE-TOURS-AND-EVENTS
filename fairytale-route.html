A storybook road brought to life

Germany’s Fairytale Route (Deutsche Märchenstraße) links places connected to the Brothers Grimm and beloved legends. Wander half-timbered towns, castles and forest paths that feel straight out of a tale.

Two elegant ways to explore

1) Base-and-daytrips around a central stretch of the route; 2) Or connect highlight towns over 3–5 days. We keep it relaxed with scenic walks, cafés and viewpoints.

Perfect combinations

Pair the Fairytale Route with the charming Half-Timbered Route for classic façades and romantic squares. It also works beautifully with Castles & Gardens.

  • Theme: Grimm heritage, forest paths, castles and half-timbered towns.
  • Best season: Spring–autumn for greenery; winter brings a quieter, misty mood.
  • Style: Gentle pace • curated photo stops • cafés & viewpoints.
  • Custom: Private, tailor-made itineraries to match your time and interests.
