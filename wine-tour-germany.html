Wine Tours in Germany

Mosel, Rhine, Rheingau, Nahe, Ahr, Franconia, Baden — plus the German Wine Route

Two elegant ways to explore German wine country

Germany is blessed with diverse wine landscapes: steep slate terraces on the Mosel, river-view vineyards along the Rhine and Rheingau, the volcanic Ahr, the gentle hills of Nahe, the distinctive Franconia (Franken), sunny Baden and the Palatinate (Pfalz).

1) Valley-hopping itinerary

Combine 2–4 regions you love: cellar tastings, vineyard viewpoints and time to enjoy riverside towns.

2) German Wine Route — Deutsche Weinstraße

Follow the classic road through the Palatinate, dotted with half-timbered villages, wine taverns and miles of vines.

Make it unforgettable by pairing with the Castles & Gardens, the scenic Romantic Rhine, or the Half-Timbered Route for storybook towns and classic façades.
