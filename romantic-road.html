Explore at your pace

Germany’s Romantic Road (Romantische Straße) strings together storybook towns, medieval towers and scenic countryside from Würzburg to Füssen in the Bavarian Alps. We tailor the route to your time and interests—north to south or the other way around—with relaxed pacing, curated photo stops and moments for cafés and viewpoints.

Beautiful combinations

Pair it with the romantic Castles & Gardens theme, or add the Half-Timbered Route for classic façades and cobbled squares.

  • Length: 460 km from Würzburg to Füssen.
  • Towns: 29 official towns including Rothenburg, Dinkelsbühl and Nördlingen.
  • Best season: Spring for flower-filled meadows; autumn for golden roads and lively squares; winter for snow and the festive charm of village markets.
  • Custom: Private, tailor-made itineraries to match your time and interests.
