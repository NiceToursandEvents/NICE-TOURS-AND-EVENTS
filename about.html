About Me

Teacher, Event Planner & Certified Tour Guide

Hello! I'm Eunice Haake, a German-Brazilian based in Düsseldorf, Germany. My passion for hospitality and cultural exchange has led me to specialize in high-quality tours and extraordinary events.

With years of experience as an event planner and certified tour guide, I design elegant experiences across Europe that are both memorable and meaningful — whether you're a solo traveler or planning a corporate getaway.
